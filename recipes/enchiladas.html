Enchiladas

Nicaraguan Enchilada, is a delicious corn tortilla with meat and rice inside of it, served with salad.


Ingredients

  • 1 onion chopped into small pieces
  • 2 chiltomas in small pieces
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 2 chopped tomato
  • ½ kg of ground beef
  • 2 cups of fried rice
  • 1 cup with water
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 beaten egg
  • 6 corn tortillas
  • ¼ shredded cabbage
  • ¼ cup vinegar
  • ½ cup of onions
  • Salt and pepper to taste
